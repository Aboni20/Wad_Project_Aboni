Sustainable Mindset Training

Participated in the "Sustainable Mindset" training course organized by ReCreativity Social Enterprise and funded by the Erasmus+ program, held in Hollókő, Hungary—a UNESCO World Heritage site.

The course focused on sustainability and youth work, aiming to enhance understanding of sustainability and promote actionable strategies for a sustainable lifestyle both personally and professionally.

Key activities included interactive workshops, team-building exercises, sustainability-themed projects, and discussions on environmental concerns. Developed skills in designing educational programs, managing resources effectively, collaborating in diverse teams, and advocating for sustainable practices.

Engaged with international peers to foster intercultural sensitivity and expand professional networks. Received a Youthpass certificate from Erasmus+ validating the skills and competencies gained during the training.
